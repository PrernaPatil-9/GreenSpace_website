Our Philosophy

We Believe In Quality
By Design

The Green Space Designs is an end-to-end design studio with a yearly turnover of over 35cr and a dedicated team of 85-90 employees. We offer a bespoke and leading-edge approach to design — precise, highly customized interiors and furniture across residential, commercial & industrial sectors.

Built upon a shared passion for design and execution, each space embodies integrity and is created with the same care, skill and attention to detail. Every challenge is treated as an opportunity, given careful thought and consideration.
